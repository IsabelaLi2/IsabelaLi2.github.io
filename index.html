Pasta Alfredo

  • Time: 20-25 min
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredient Quantity
Fettuccine pasta 12 oz (340 g)
Heavy cream 1 cup (240 ml)
Unsalted butter 1/2 cup (115 g)
Grated Parmesan cheese 1 cup (100 g)
Step 1: Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
Step 2: In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat. Add heavy cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
Step 3: Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted. Combine with pasta and serve.

Chicken Curry

  • Time: 30-35 min
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredient Quantity
Chicken breast 1 lb (450 g), diced
Coconut milk 1 can (400 ml)
Curry powder 2 tbsp
Onion 1 medium, chopped
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Step 1: In a pot, sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
Step 2: Add diced chicken and cook until no longer pink.
Step 3: Stir in curry powder and coconut milk. Simmer for 15 minutes.

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 50-60 min
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredient Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 3/4 cups (220 g)
Cocoa powder 3/4 cup (65 g)
Sugar 2 cups (400 g)
Baking powder 1 1/2 tsp
Eggs 2 large
Milk 1 cup (240 ml)
Vegetable oil 1/2 cup (120 ml)
Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
Step 2: In a bowl, mix dry ingredients. In another bowl, combine wet ingredients.
Step 3: Combine both mixtures and pour into a greased cake pan. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
